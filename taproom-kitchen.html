White onion, finely diced3 (~600g)
Carrots, finely diced3 (~300g)
Celery, finely diced4 sticks (~200g)
Garlic cloves, minced8 (~40g)
Tomato paste200g
Tinned chopped tomatoes800g
Red wine750ml
Beef stock1L
Bay leaves4
Fresh thyme6 sprigs
  1. Season and sear shin and cheek in batches — deep mahogany. Remove.
  2. Brown crumbled sausage in same pan. Remove, reserve.
  3. Soffritto: onion, carrot, celery 15 mins until soft. Add garlic 2 mins.
  4. Cook tomato paste 3 mins. Deglaze with wine, reduce by half. Add tomatoes, stock, herbs. Return all meat.
