Best New 2024 Japanese Ramen

Akahoshi Ramen

Chicago, IL

Chef Mike Satinover spent 12 years posting about broth on r/ramen before opening. Uses a refractometer to measure broth viscosity. Obsession as a founding story.

Known for
  • Miso ramen
  • Broth measured to tolerance
  • Hand-pulled noodles
Summer Menu Take

The obsessive perfectionist origin story is irresistible. For summer: cold ramen (hiyashi chuka) or dipping ramen (tsukemen) using the same intensely crafted broth, served chilled. Craft over convenience. The backstory of how a dish was perfected is the best marketing you have.
