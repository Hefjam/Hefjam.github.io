Who we share your data with

We use a small number of trusted services to run this app. Each acts as a data processor on our behalf:

  • Resend — email delivery for your receipt (resend.com)
  • Stripe — payment processing (stripe.com)
  • EposNow — point-of-sale system that receives your order (eposnow.com)
  • Cloudflare — hosting and security for the app (cloudflare.com)
  • GitHub — static file hosting for the app (github.com)

We do not sell your data. We do not share it with anyone else.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — ask us to delete your data (where no legal obligation to keep it exists)
  • Restriction — ask us to limit how we use your data
  • Objection — object to us processing your data on legitimate interests grounds
  • Withdraw consent — unsubscribe from marketing at any time by emailing us

To exercise any of these rights, email info@thetaproom.co.uk. We will respond within 30 days.

Cookies and tracking

This app does not use cookies or any tracking technology. It does not use Google Analytics or any advertising trackers. Your session order history is stored temporarily in your browser's memory only and is cleared when you close the tab.
